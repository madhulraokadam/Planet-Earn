FAQ
How is a Sparklomat different from regular recycling containers?

Our Sparklomats motivate users to donate more bottles through a progressive rewards systems and gaming elements. By making recycling instantly more satisfying, we create lasting habits that encourage more people to adopt a more sustainable lifestyle.

What happens to plastic and aluminium bottles after they enter a Sparklomat?

They get pressed and later transported to recycling facilities where they get sorted, cleaned and transformed into raw materials that can be used for the production of packaging, consumer goods and many other cool things.

How can I get rewards for donating bottles?

You need to download our app, complete a short registration and start receiving bonuses and rewards from our partners every time you donate a bottle.
